Frequently Asked Questions

What is PicMagic.art?

PicMagic.art is an AI-powered photo editing app that allows you to edit your photos by simply describing the changes you want. Just upload a photo, type or speak your instructions, and let AI do the magic!

What kind of edits can the AI do?

PicMagic.art can handle various edits, including:

  • Removing or changing backgrounds
  • Retouching faces and enhancing images
  • Adding or removing objects
  • Applying artistic styles and filters
  • Adjusting colors, lighting, and more
How does the credit system work?

Each edit you make on PicMagic.art costs one credit. You can purchase credits in bundles, and there may also be free credits available for new users.

How long does an edit take?

Most edits are completed within a few seconds to a minute, depending on the complexity of the request.

Will my photos be saved on PicMagic.art?

For privacy, your uploaded images are processed and stored temporarily. You can download your edited images, and they will be removed from our servers after a short period.

What file formats are supported?

We currently support JPG, PNG, DNG, and WEBP formats for uploads.
