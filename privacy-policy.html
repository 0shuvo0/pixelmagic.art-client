Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 15/03/2025

At PicMagic.art, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email, username, and password.
  • Uploaded Images: Images you upload are processed by AI and stored temporarily.
  • Payment Data: Payments are processed by third-party providers; we do not store payment details.
  • Usage Data: We collect analytics to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI-powered editing service.
  • To process transactions and purchases.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates and support.

3. Data Retention

  • Uploaded images are stored temporarily and deleted after processing.
  • Account information is stored as long as you have an active account.
  • You can request data deletion by contacting us.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We may share information with trusted third-party services for processing payments and analytics.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose data if required by law.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. However, third-party services we use may employ cookies to enhance their services.

6. Security Measures

We implement encryption and security protocols to protect user data, but no system is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

PicMagic.art is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after updates means you accept the changes.
